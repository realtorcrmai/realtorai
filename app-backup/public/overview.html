Automation
Follow-Ups That
Never Get Forgotten
Seven workflow sequences run automatically behind the scenes. When a new lead comes in, speed-to-contact fires a text within sixty seconds. After a closing, the post-close workflow sends congratulations, schedules check-ins at thirty, sixty, and ninety days, and asks for a referral at the right moment.
  • Speed-to-Contact — respond in under 60 seconds
  • Buyer Nurture — 30-day education drip
  • Open House Follow-Up — same-day to one week
  • Post-Close Buyer — closing day to annual
  • Post-Close Seller — thank you to referral ask
  • Lead Re-Engagement — wake up cold leads
  • Referral Partner — keep your network warm

Canadian Compliance
BC Forms,
Zero Paperwork Stress
Every required BC real estate form is built right into the platform. FINTRAC identification, disclosure of representation, property disclosure statements, listing contracts, and purchase agreements. Fields auto-populate from your contact and listing data. Edit in the PDF viewer, save drafts, and track completion across every listing.
