# AI Content Engine

## Problem Statement

Marketing a property listing today requires professional video walkthroughs, styled photography, compelling MLS remarks, and social media captions with hashtags — all tailored to each property's unique features. Most BC realtors either spend thousands on production crews for every listing or settle for amateur content that does not stand out. There is a painful gap between what buyers expect to see online and what agents can produce affordably at scale. RealtorAI's AI Content Engine generates listing videos, lifestyle images, MLS remarks, and Instagram captions automatically from your property details and photos, giving every listing premium-quality marketing content without the production overhead.

## Business Value

Professional marketing content is the single biggest factor in online listing engagement. Listings with video get 403% more inquiries than those without. The AI Content Engine lets you produce 4K walkthrough videos, styled images, polished MLS remarks, and social media posts for every listing in minutes instead of days, at a fraction of the cost of traditional production. Consistent, high-quality content across all your listings elevates your brand and attracts more buyer attention.

## Where to Find It

- Click **Content** in the top navigation bar to open the content dashboard at /content
- The dashboard shows listing cards with status indicators for prompts, video, and images
- Click any listing card to open its content detail page at /content/[id]
- The detail page has sections for property highlights, video generation, image generation, MLS remarks, and social captions

## Preconditions

- You must be logged in
- The listing must exist with property details (address, bedrooms, bathrooms, price, features)
- For video generation: Kling AI API must be configured and the listing must have at least one hero image
- For image generation: Kling AI Image API must be configured
- For MLS remarks and social captions: Anthropic API key must be configured

## Core Workflow

1. Navigate to /content and click a listing card to open its detail page
2. Write a property highlights prompt describing the key features, neighbourhood, and selling points
3. Click Generate Video to create a 4K walkthrough from the hero image
4. Click Generate Images to create lifestyle and neighbourhood images from text prompts
5. Monitor generation status (Pending → Processing → Completed)
6. Review the generated video and images when processing completes
7. Generate MLS public remarks (auto-written, max 500 characters, editable)
8. Generate MLS REALTOR remarks (auto-written, max 500 characters, editable)
9. Generate Instagram caption and hashtags
10. Download completed assets for MLS submission and social media posting

## End-to-End Scenarios

### Scenario: Generate complete marketing content for a new listing

- **Role:** Listing Agent
- **Goal:** Create video, images, MLS remarks, and social content for a property that just went live
- **Navigation:** Click Content > click the listing card
- **Steps:**
  1. Open the content detail page for the listing
  2. Write property highlights: "Stunning 3-bed corner unit in Burnaby Heights with floor-to-ceiling windows, mountain views, gourmet kitchen with quartz countertops, and a private rooftop patio"
  3. Save the highlights prompt
  4. Click Generate Video — the system sends the hero image to Kling AI for 4K video creation
  5. Click Generate Images — the system creates lifestyle images from the property description
  6. While video and images are processing, click Generate MLS Remarks
  7. Review the auto-generated public remarks (500 characters max) and edit if needed
  8. Review the auto-generated REALTOR remarks and edit if needed
  9. Click Generate Social Caption for Instagram-ready text with hashtags
  10. Return later to download completed video and images
- **Expected outcome:** Full marketing package: 4K video, lifestyle images, MLS remarks, and Instagram caption
- **Edge cases:** Video generation fails due to API quota; hero image is low resolution
- **Common mistakes:** Writing vague highlights that produce generic content
- **Recovery:** Rewrite the highlights with specific, detailed property features and regenerate

### Scenario: Generate MLS remarks for a listing approaching submission

- **Role:** Listing Agent
- **Goal:** Get polished public and REALTOR remarks ready for MLS submission
- **Navigation:** Click Content > click the listing card
- **Steps:**
  1. Open the content detail page
  2. Ensure property highlights are written (these guide the AI's writing)
  3. Click Generate MLS Remarks in the MLS section
  4. Review the public remarks — verify accuracy of features, measurements, and neighbourhood references
  5. Edit the text directly if any changes are needed
  6. Verify the character count is at or below 500
  7. Review the REALTOR remarks and edit similarly
  8. Copy the finalized remarks for use in MLS submission
- **Expected outcome:** Two sets of MLS remarks (public + REALTOR), each under 500 characters, accurate and polished
- **Edge cases:** The AI includes features that are not actually present; remarks exceed 500 characters
- **Common mistakes:** Not reviewing AI-generated remarks for accuracy before submission
- **Recovery:** Edit the remarks directly in the text field; the character counter helps you stay within the 500-character limit

## Step-by-Step Procedures

### Procedure: Write property highlights for AI generation

- **When to use:** Before generating any content for a listing — highlights guide all AI output
- **Starting point:** /content/[id] detail page
- **Steps:**
  1. Open the listing's content detail page
  2. Find the Property Highlights section
  3. Write a detailed description of the property's best features: layout, views, finishes, neighbourhood, proximity to transit/schools
  4. Be specific: "quartz countertops" is better than "nice kitchen"; "10-minute walk to SkyTrain" is better than "close to transit"
  5. Include the target buyer profile if relevant (e.g., "ideal for young families" or "perfect downsizer")
  6. Save the highlights
- **Validations:** Highlights should be at least 2-3 sentences for meaningful AI output
- **What happens next:** The highlights are saved and used as context for all subsequent AI generation (video prompts, image prompts, MLS remarks, social captions)
- **Common mistakes:** Writing one-sentence highlights that produce generic, unspecific content
- **Recovery:** Expand the highlights with more property-specific details and regenerate content

### Procedure: Generate and download an AI video

- **When to use:** You want a 4K walkthrough video for Instagram Reels or listing marketing
- **Starting point:** /content/[id] detail page with property highlights written
- **Steps:**
  1. Ensure the listing has at least one hero image uploaded
  2. Open the content detail page
  3. Click Generate Video
  4. The system sends the hero image and property context to Kling AI
  5. Status changes to Pending, then Processing
  6. Wait for processing to complete (this can take several minutes)
  7. When status shows Completed, preview the video
  8. Click Download to save the 4K video file
- **Validations:** Hero image must be uploaded; Kling AI API must be configured; highlights should be written
- **What happens next:** The video is available for download in 9:16 aspect ratio (optimized for Instagram Reels and TikTok). You can also regenerate if the result is not satisfactory.
- **Common mistakes:** Clicking Generate Video without a hero image uploaded
- **Recovery:** Upload a hero image to the listing first, then return to the content page and generate

### Procedure: Generate MLS remarks

- **When to use:** You need public and REALTOR remarks for MLS submission
- **Starting point:** /content/[id] detail page
- **Steps:**
  1. Ensure property highlights are written
  2. Scroll to the MLS Remarks section
  3. Click Generate to create public remarks
  4. Review the auto-generated text (max 500 characters)
  5. Edit as needed — adjust tone, add specifics, correct any inaccuracies
  6. Verify character count is within the 500-character limit
  7. The REALTOR remarks are generated separately — review and edit those as well
  8. Save both sets of remarks
- **Validations:** Character count must not exceed 500 for either remarks field
- **What happens next:** Remarks are saved to the listing record and ready for MLS submission in Phase 7 of the listing workflow
- **Common mistakes:** Not verifying property details mentioned in the AI-generated remarks
- **Recovery:** Compare remarks against the actual listing record and correct any discrepancies

## Edge Cases

1. **Hero image is too low resolution:** Kling AI may produce a blurry or artifacted video. Upload a higher-resolution image (recommended: at least 1920x1080) and regenerate.
2. **Video generation stuck in Processing for over 30 minutes:** The Kling AI task may have failed silently. The system polls /api/kling/status for updates. Try regenerating the video after waiting.
3. **MLS remarks exceed 500 characters after editing:** The character counter on the form prevents submission over the limit. Trim the text until it fits within 500 characters.
4. **Property highlights are empty when generating content:** The AI generates from listing data alone, but quality is significantly lower. Always write highlights first for the best results.
5. **Kling AI API quota exhausted:** Generation requests will fail. Check your Kling AI account for quota status and wait for the reset period, or upgrade your plan.
6. **Two agents generate content for the same listing simultaneously:** Each generation creates a new task. The most recently completed version is shown. This is not harmful but wastes API calls.
7. **Listing details change after content was generated:** Previously generated remarks and captions are not automatically updated. Regenerate content after significant listing changes (price update, new photos).

## FAQ

### How long does video generation take?

Video generation typically takes 3-10 minutes depending on Kling AI server load. The status indicator on the content page updates automatically from Pending to Processing to Completed. You do not need to stay on the page — come back and check later.

### Can I generate content for a listing that does not have photos yet?

You can generate MLS remarks and social captions without photos, as long as property highlights are written. However, video generation requires at least one hero image, and image generation works best with detailed property and neighbourhood descriptions.

### What aspect ratio are the generated videos?

Videos are generated in 9:16 (portrait) at 4K resolution, optimized for Instagram Reels and TikTok. This is the standard format for vertical social media video content.

### Can I regenerate content if I do not like the first result?

Yes. Click the Generate button again for any content type (video, images, remarks, captions) to create a new version. The previous version is replaced by the new generation.

### Are there costs associated with AI content generation?

Video and image generation use the Kling AI API, which has usage-based pricing. MLS remarks and social captions use the Anthropic Claude API. Check your API accounts for current usage and billing. RealtorAI does not add additional charges beyond the API costs.

### Can I use the generated video directly on MLS?

The 9:16 video format is designed for social media platforms. Some MLS systems accept video uploads — check your local MLS requirements for format and size limits. You may need to convert the video to a different aspect ratio for MLS if they require landscape format.

### How do I write good property highlights for the best AI output?

Be specific and detailed. Instead of "nice condo," write "Bright south-facing 2BR corner unit with 9-foot ceilings, engineered hardwood throughout, quartz island kitchen with Bosch appliances, and a 200 sqft covered balcony overlooking the North Shore mountains." Include neighbourhood highlights: transit proximity, school catchments, parks, and local amenities. The more specific your input, the more compelling the AI output.

## Escalation Guidance

**Fix yourself:**
- Generic AI output — rewrite property highlights with more specific details and regenerate
- Character count over 500 — trim the MLS remarks text
- Video quality is poor — upload a higher-resolution hero image and regenerate
- Wrong listing selected — navigate to the correct listing's content page

**Needs admin:**
- Kling AI API key is invalid or quota exceeded — requires environment variable update or account upgrade
- Anthropic API key not working — requires environment variable verification
- Content page consistently fails to load — requires front-end investigation
- Generated video URLs expire or become inaccessible — requires investigation of asset storage

**Information to gather before escalating:**
- The listing address and ID
- Which content type failed (video, image, remarks, caption)
- The exact error message or unexpected behavior
- The property highlights text you used
- Screenshots of the content detail page and any error states
