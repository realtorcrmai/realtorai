PROPERTY DISCLOSURE STATEMENT
Residential · Form 5A
NOTE: This statement is not a warranty. Sellers must disclose known material latent defects. Buyers should conduct their own independent inspections.
Date: March 11, 2026
INSTRUCTIONS: For each question, check Yes, No, or Unknown. Provide details in the space provided for any "Yes" answer.
A. TITLE / LEGAL ISSUES
A1 Are there any easements, rights of way, or other encumbrances registered against the property title, other than those shown on the certificate of title?
A2 Are you aware of any unregistered encumbrances, claims, or disputes affecting the property?
A3 Is the property subject to any rental agreement, tenancy agreement, or lease that will survive closing?
A4 Are you aware of any work orders, by-law violations, or municipal notices outstanding against the property?
A5 Are there any pending or threatened legal proceedings affecting the property?
B. WATER / SEWAGE
B1 Is the property connected to a municipal water system?
B2 Is the property connected to a municipal sewage system?
B3 Are you aware of any problems with the water supply, water pressure, or water quality?
B4 Has the property ever been connected to a well or septic system?
B5 Are you aware of any moisture or water infiltration in the basement, crawl space, or any other area of the property?

C. STRUCTURE / ROOF
C1 Are you aware of any structural problems, settling, or movement affecting the foundations, walls, or roof?
C2 Has any part of the property been damaged by fire, flood, or other causes?
C3 Are you aware of any asbestos-containing materials in the property?
C4 Are you aware of any problems with the roof (leaking, improper installation, etc.)?
C5 Have there been any renovations, additions, or alterations to the property without a required permit?
D. ELECTRICAL / HEATING / COOLING
D1 Are you aware of any problems with the electrical system (wiring, panel, knob and tube, aluminum wiring)?
D2 Are you aware of any problems with the heating system (furnace, boiler, heat pump)?
D3 Does the property have a wood-burning fireplace or stove?
D4 Is the property connected to natural gas?
D5 Are you aware of any problems with the cooling system?
E. ENVIRONMENTAL / SAFETY
E1 Is there any known soil contamination, underground storage tanks, or environmental hazards?
E2 Has the property been used as a marijuana grow operation or drug lab at any time?
E3 Are you aware of any lead paint, vermiculite insulation, or urea formaldehyde foam insulation?
E4 Has the property been tested for radon gas?
E5 Are there any known safety hazards (unguarded pools, unsafe decks, etc.)?
F. GENERAL
F1 Are you aware of any material latent defects in the property not covered in the above sections?
F2 Is there a homeowner warranty on the property?
F3 Has the property had a professional home inspection in the last 3 years?

SELLER DECLARATION
The Seller(s) represent(s) that the information provided in this Property Disclosure Statement is accurate to the best of the Seller's knowledge as of the date signed below. The Seller(s) acknowledge(s) that this statement is not a warranty and that the Seller(s) is/are obligated to disclose material latent defects known to the Seller(s) that would not be apparent on a reasonable inspection.
