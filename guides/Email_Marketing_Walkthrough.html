The Big Picture

Your AI agent manages email relationships with every contact — buyers, sellers, and past clients — automatically.

🤖

AI Writes Every Email

Claude AI generates unique, personalized emails for each contact using their CRM data, click history, and preferences. No templates, no mail merge — every email is one-of-a-kind.

📊

Clicks Teach the System

When a contact clicks a listing, opens a market report, or ignores an email — the AI learns. Next time, it sends exactly what they want.

🔥

Hot Lead Alerts

When someone clicks "Book a Showing" or views a listing 4 times — you get an instant alert. Call them before they call another agent.

🛡️

You Stay in Control

Start in Ghost Mode (AI drafts, you review everything). Gradually give it more autonomy as it proves itself. Pause anytime with one click.

How It's Different From Mailchimp

Mailchimp sends the same campaign to a segment. ListingFlow's AI writes a unique email for each person based on everything it knows about them — their search history, click patterns, engagement level, and where they are in the buying/selling journey.

Trust Levels — You're Always in Control

The AI earns your trust gradually. You control the pace.

LevelWhat HappensYour EffortWhen
👻 Ghost AI drafts silently. Nothing sends. You compare later. Zero — just observe Week 1-2
🤝 Copilot AI drafts go to your approval queue. One-tap to send. ~5 min/day reviewing emails Week 3-4
👁️ Supervised AI sends automatically. You can recall within 30 minutes. Daily digest review Month 2-3
🚀 Autonomous AI handles everything. You get weekly summary + hot lead alerts. Weekly glance Month 4+

Promotion is Always in Your Hands

The system will suggest promotion when your edit rate drops below 15% and approval rate exceeds 50% after 20+ emails. But YOU choose when to upgrade. You can also downgrade or pause anytime with one click.

🛑

Emergency Controls

Pause All: One click stops all sending. AI keeps drafting but nothing goes out.
Never Email: Flag any contact to permanently exclude them.
Recall: Undo any sent email within 30 minutes (Supervised mode).
Per-Contact Override: Set trust level individually for sensitive contacts.
