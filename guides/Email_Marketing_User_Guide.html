1

Contact enters your CRM

A new buyer or seller is added — manually or from a lead form. The AI automatically enrolls them in a personalized email journey.

2

AI writes a unique email

Claude reads their profile, preferences, area of interest, and recent activity. It writes an email that feels personally written — because it is, just by AI.

3

You review & approve

The email appears in your approval queue. Preview it, edit if needed, then click "Send." Or turn on auto mode and let the AI handle everything.

4

Contact opens & clicks

Every open and click is tracked. The AI learns what each contact cares about — which areas, property types, and content they engage with.

5

Next email adapts

The AI uses click data to write better emails next time. A contact who clicks school info gets family-focused content. One who clicks market data gets stats-heavy updates.

6

You get hot lead alerts

When a contact clicks "Book a Showing" or views a CMA, you get an instant notification. They're ready to act — call them now.
